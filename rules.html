SERVER RULES

Breaking these rules may result in a ban or mute

General Rules

Be respectful to all players and staff members
No racism, sexism, homophobia, or other forms of discrimination
No spamming or excessive use of caps in chat
No advertising other servers or services
No inappropriate usernames or skins

Gameplay Rules

NO HACKING - Any use of hacked clients, x-ray, or unfair advantages is prohibited
NO SCAMMING - Do not attempt to scam other players of their items or currency
NO GRIEFING - Do not destroy other players' builds without permission
NO GRIEFING SPAWN - The spawn area is protected, do not attempt to modify it
No exploiting bugs or glitches
No AFK farming without staff approval

PvP Rules

No spawn killing or camping
No teaming in solo PvP modes
Combat logging is prohibited

Voice Chat Rules (If applicable)

No excessive screaming or loud noises
No playing music or sounds through voice chat
Keep voice chat appropriate (same standards as text chat)
These rules are not exhaustive. Staff members have final say in rule interpretations.
